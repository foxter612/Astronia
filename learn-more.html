Short History:

Astronia was founded on 22nd December 2024 by its King/Khalifa, Mahadi Al Mahmud. The country was established with the goal of being an Islamic kingdom and state, and it is developing toward that goal. The host country of Astronia is Bangladesh, and it is located within this nation. The first citizen of Astronia is the founder, who is currently the King/Khalifa. The first real citizen (excluding the founder) is Afnan.
